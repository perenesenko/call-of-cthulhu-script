# Haunted House

A classic Call of Cthulhu scenario set in a crumbling New England estate.

## Overview

The investigators are called to the Blackwood estate after its elderly owner, Professor Elias Blackwood, vanishes without a trace. Locals whisper of strange lights, unearthly sounds, and a darkness that predates the house itself.

## Structure

1. Introduction – The investigators arrive at Blackwood estate.
2. Exploration – They search the house and uncover disturbing clues.
3. Climax – A confrontation with the horror lurking in the basement.
